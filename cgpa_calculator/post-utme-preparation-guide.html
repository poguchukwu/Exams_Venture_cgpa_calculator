Post-UTME Preparation Guide for Nigerian Students

Last Updated: April 2026

Post-UTME is where many admission battles are won or lost. A good strategy can push your total ranking above many students with similar JAMB scores.

1. Build a 4-Week Focus Plan

  • Week 1: full syllabus map and baseline test
  • Week 2: weak-topic intensive drills
  • Week 3: timed past-question sessions
  • Week 4: mock tests and revision polish

2. Use Time-Boxed Practice

Practice with strict timing to simulate test pressure. This improves speed and lowers panic during the real screening exam.

3. Optimize Accuracy First, Then Speed

Avoid rushing early. Build accurate solving patterns first, then gradually increase speed across repeated mock sessions.

4. Day-Before Exam Checklist

  • Review formulas and key facts only
  • Sleep early and avoid last-minute cramming
  • Confirm venue logistics and required documents

5. After Admission

Once admitted, begin semester performance tracking immediately with the Exams Venture CGPA calculator so you maintain momentum into your first year.
